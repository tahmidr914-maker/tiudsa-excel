# Unit 10 – Text & Date Functions

## Objective
This assignment demonstrates the use of Excel text and date functions to manipulate employee information from an HR dataset. The task includes extracting text, calculating employment duration, and classifying employees based on years of service.

## Dataset
- HRDataset_v141.xlsx

## Text Functions Used
- LEFT() – Extracted the first three letters of employee names.
- RIGHT() – Extracted the last three letters of employee names.
- TEXTJOIN() – Combined employee name fields into a full name.

## Date Functions Used
- YEAR() – Extracted the hire year.
- MONTH() – Extracted the hire month.
- TODAY() – Displayed the current date dynamically.
- DATEDIF() – Calculated years of service.

## Employee Classification
Employees were categorized using the following criteria:
- New: Less than 1 year of service.
- Mid: 1–3 years of service.
- Senior: More than 3 years of service.

## Skills Demonstrated
- Text manipulation
- Date calculations
- Dynamic date functions
- Employee tenure analysis
- Logical classification using IFS()
